# Tamilnadu Textile Exchange - PRD

## Original Problem Statement
Landing page for Tamilnadu Textile Exchange - a textile sourcing intensive program in South India. Meet mills. See real ex-mill pricing. Build your supplier map—without middlemen. 15 people · 12 days · $4,500.

## User Choices
- Dark industrial aesthetic reflecting textile mills
- Google Form link for applications (no database)
- High-converting layout with all key sections
- No external integrations
- Authentic textile mill imagery

## Target Audience
- Fashion & apparel brand founders
- Textile family business heirs
- Sourcing heads & buying managers
- Professors with student cohorts
